## So, who are we?

Neon Carrot is made up of Caleb (that's me!) and Carol - partners in business and in ~~crime~~ life. The company was formed in 2020, amidst a global pandemic that upended modern society and catapulted hidden social inequities into view. During that wild time, we figured we could use our skills in IT and project management to actually help people - _real_ people with _real_ problems. And what started out as a passion project that we contributed to on the side has now evolved into our full-time vocation and top priority!
